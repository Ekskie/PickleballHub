Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about Pickleball Reservations? We've got answers.

How do I make a pickleball reservation on PickleballHub?

Simply enter your location in the search bar or click "Find Courts" in the menu. Browse the available times, select your slot, and secure it with integrated GCash payments.

How does player matchmaking work?

When you sign up, you specify your experience level. You can search the community feed for matches or create public court bookings that other players of similar ratings can join.

What is the weather refund policy?

If you make a reservation at an outdoor court and it rains, you can request a booking cancellation. Our system automatically processes refunds or credits to your account.
